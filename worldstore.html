How to add your products

This is the fastest “no database” method for GitHub Pages.

1) Put images in a folder

Create a folder like /images and place your product photos inside.

Example: images/product-01.jpg

2) Duplicate a product card

Copy one <article class="store-card"> block and paste it again.

Change title, price, description, image path.

3) Add your checkout link

Replace https://your-checkout-link.com with your PayPal/Stripe/Gumroad link.

That’s it — customers can buy immediately.
